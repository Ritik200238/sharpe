# judge.md — What the Judges Score

**Hackathon:** TxODDS World Cup (Superteam Earn) · **Track 2 — Trading Tools & Agents**

> This page is our scorecard. Everything we build is measured against it. Build to hit all 5.

---

## 1. The 5 Official Judging Criteria

| # | What they score | What it means for us |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Core Functionality & Data Ingestion** | It actually runs and reads live TxLINE feeds smoothly. |
| 2 | **Autonomous Operation** | Runs 100% on its own after you start it. Zero manual input. |
| 3 | **Logic & Code Architecture** | The strategy is clean, deterministic, and explainable — not a black box. |
| 4 | **Innovation & Novelty** | A genuinely new idea, not a copy of their example ideas. |
| 5 | **Production Readiness** | A pro trading desk could really deploy it. Robust, doesn't crash. |

---

## 2. The Hidden Requirement (implied, not written)

**The agent must create real value.**

Ask: *If this agent ran 24/7, would anyone care?*

- ❌ **Weak:** "It just prints the score." → Not enough.
- ✅ **Strong:** It **identifies opportunities, summarizes important events, detects anomalies, or automates a task people currently do manually.**

---

## 3. Extra Signals Judges Reward

- **Product–Market Fit** — clear potential to attract and retain users.
- **Innovation & Creativity** — original approaches that push boundaries.
